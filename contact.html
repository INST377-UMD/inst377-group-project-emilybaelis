FAQs

How do I start the adoption process?

You can start by filling out our online application form to express your interest in adopting a pet.

What are the adoption fees?

Adoption fees vary depending on the pet's age, breed, and medical needs. Please contact us for more details.

How long does the adoption process take?

The process can vary for each animal, but it typically takes one to two weeks from application to bringing your new pet home.

What if I have other pets at home already?

We recommend a meet-and-greet with all household pets to ensure compatibility before finalizing the adoption.

Are the pets vaccinated and spayed/neutered before adoption?

Yes, all our pets are vaccinated, spayed/neutered, and receive a health check before being available for adoption.
